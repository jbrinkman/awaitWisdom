St. Louis Days of .NET 2013

Published on Sunday, October 27, 2013
Migrated Speaking Tech

St. Louis Days of .NET (STLDODN) 2013 is fast approaching. Polaris Solutions is a sponsor this year and I'll be presenting there with two of my colleagues.

So check out check out STLDODN to get signed up and be sure to check us out. The detailed agenda isn't posted yet, but should be available soon.
